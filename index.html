About

The Spark

A Journey Begins

My name is H.M. Pasindu Diwakara. My passion for design began with a simple question: "Why?" I've always been fascinated by the technology we use every day and how great design can make it feel effortless. This curiosity ignited my passion for creating intuitive, accessible, and truly useful user experiences.

The Foundation

Building a Designer's Mindset

I am currently a BICT (Hons) student at Rajarata University of Sri Lanka. This academic journey has given me a deep understanding of the technical side of development and a critical, problem-solving mindset, helping me blend logic and creativity to ensure my work is both visually compelling and functionally sound.

The Real-World Impact

From Theory to Practice

During my internship at "LankaWeb Innovations" in Colombo, I put my skills to the test. I contributed to real-world projects, conducted user interviews, and prototyped solutions that directly addressed client needs. This experience taught me the value of teamwork and showed me firsthand how user-centric design drives meaningful business results.

Today & Tomorrow

The Story Continues

Today, I am a dedicated UI/UX designer, constantly learning and refining my craft. I am passionate about creating digital products that make a positive impact and am excited to continue my journey, collaborate with passionate teams, and take on new challenges to grow as a designer.
